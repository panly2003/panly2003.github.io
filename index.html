👋 About Me

Hi! My name is Leyi Pan (潘乐怡). I am currently a 2nd-year Ph.D. student at the School of Software, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Lijie Wen. I received my B.Eng. from the same school in 2024.

I'm currently a research intern on reasoning, reinforcement learning and general agents at Tongyi Lab, Alibaba Group (mentored by Dr. Yunpeng Zhai). Previously I interned at Zhipu AI (Dr. Shiyu Huang) and served as Research Assistant at CUHK MISC Lab (Prof. Irwin King, ACM/IEEE/AAAI Fellow).
